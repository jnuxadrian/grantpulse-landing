Who is GrantPulse built for?

GrantPulse is the right fit for you if:

  • You manage 2-4 active grants (not dozens)
  • You're looking for simplicity over complexity
  • Your team is small enough to fit in Slack but needs a shared source of truth
  • You want to spend time on your mission, not managing spreadsheets
  • You're willing to share feedback as we build
div>

When we're probably not the right fit.

If you need enterprise compliance features, integrations with 10 systems, or advanced audit workflows today—we're probably not the right fit yet. And that's okay. As we grow, we'll build that. But right now, we're optimizing for simplicity and clarity for small teams.
